The Aesthetic

Vintage dressing is memory made wearable. It rejects the disposable in favour of the durable — pieces that carry provenance, texture, and a quiet resistance to the new.

The Wardrobe

Levi 501s. Silk slip dresses. Collegiate crewnecks. Grandpa cardigans. Dead stock military surplus. The denim jacket that fits like it was made for you — even though it wasn't.

The Reference

Winona Ryder, 1993. Stevie Nicks, always. The corner of a thrift store on a Tuesday. A photograph with light-leak edges and faded colour.
